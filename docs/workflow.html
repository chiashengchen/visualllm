Where the 8-second budget goes

Per-turn delay against the 8 s acceptance bar. The streaming design means stages overlap — so end-to-end TTFO ≈ VAD + LLM, not the sum of every stage.

0 s2 s4 s6 s8 s
The LLM hop (~0.9–1.4 s) is the single largest contributor — it carries the transpacific round-trip to OpenRouter's US endpoint. It is the strongest argument for an Asia-region LLM endpoint. TTS (0.22 s) and the local avatar render hide inside the LLM's window, so they cost almost nothing on the critical path.
